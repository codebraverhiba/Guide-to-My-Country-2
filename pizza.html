Authentic Italian Margherita Pizza Recipe

Prep Time: 1.5–2 hours (includes dough rising)

Servings: 2 medium pizzas

ingredients

  1. For the Dough:
    • 2 cups (250 g) all-purpose flour
    • ¾ cup (180 ml) warm water
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp dry yeast (or 10 g fresh yeast)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. For the Topping:
    • ½ cup (120 ml) tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
    • 1 cup (100 g) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil (for drizzling)
    • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a small bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy.
    • In a large bowl, mix flour and salt.
    • Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, then knead for about 8–10 minutes until smooth.
    • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rise for 1 hour (it should double in size).
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • If using canned tomatoes, blend them with a pinch of salt and a drizzle of olive oil.
    • Simmer for 10 minutes (optional) for richer flavor.
  3. Shape the Pizza:
    • Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F) — as hot as it goes!
    • Divide the dough into two balls.
    • On a floured surface, stretch each one into a thin circle (about 10–12 inches).
    • Transfer to a baking tray or pizza stone.
  4. Add the Toppings
    • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
    • Add mozzarella slices evenly.
    • Place fresh basil leaves on top.
    • Drizzle lightly with olive oil.
  5. Bake:
    • Bake for 8–12 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
    • If you have a pizza stone, the crust will be crispier and more authentic!
